About

The Cane River Historic Inventory and Map is created using Arches, an open source software platform that is built to inventory and help manage all types of immovable cultural heritage. This deployment of Arches has been customized to reflect the unique character of the Cane River National Heritage Area. Find out more about Arches here. Ongoing support for inventory comes from the National Center for Preservation Technology and Training.
